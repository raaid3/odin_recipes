How to make butter chicken

Description

This recipe will tell you how to make a ridch, fragrant, and spicy Indian butter chicken like no other. The recipe has been put together by several top chefs in India and is a must-try!

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 pounds of skinless, boneless chicken
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 cup yogurt

Steps

  1. Make marinade with first few ingredients
  2. Refrigerate the marinate for 3 to 4 hours
  3. Use some magic spells
  4. Voila! It's ready!
