Burrito Recipe

Description

This recipe is a guide on how you can make a delicious fajita burrito right from the comfort of your home. This recipe is so simple and easy that not trying it would feel like a crime.

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces of skinless, boneless, chicken breast.
  • 1/2 onion
  • 5 ounces multi-colored pepper strips
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • anything else you want

Steps

  1. Place the ingredients in a resealable plastic bag and seal it. Then squeeze it.
  2. Preheat the oven to atleast 5000 degrees Celsius. No, this is not a typo, I really do mean 5000 degrees Celsius. This is a very important part of the process.
  3. Toss your ingredients into the volcano of an oven.
  4. Use long metal tongs to pull your final dish out. It may look more like molten lava than a burrito but trust me, it tastes like heaven.
