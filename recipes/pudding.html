How to make a pudding

Description

Who doesn't love a tasty, soft, and melt-in-mouth pudding?. Using only a few ingredients, you can make this delicious pudding right from home. Nothing beats a homemade pudding!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat the milk until bubles form at the edges
  2. Mix the sugar, cornstarch, and salt together in a small bowl.
  3. Add the sugar mixture to the milk, and a little at a time, stirring until thick.
  4. Remove the pudding from the heat, then stir in the butter and vanilla.
